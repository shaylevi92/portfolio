Overview

iPlant is a comprehensive IoT solution designed to revolutionize plant care in nurseries and greenhouses. The system combines advanced sensors with an intuitive dashboard interface, enabling precise monitoring and control of crucial plant growth parameters.

The project addresses key challenges in modern plant care:

  • Real-time monitoring of vital plant metrics
  • Automated watering and feeding systems
  • Smart alerts and notifications
  • Data-driven growth optimization

By integrating IoT technology with user-centered design, iPlant makes professional-grade plant care accessible to both commercial nurseries and home gardeners.

Research

Our research phase began with extensive interviews with nursery owners, home gardeners, and plant care professionals. Through user interviews and market analysis, we identified several critical pain points:

  • Inconsistent growing conditions leading to plant health issues
  • Time-consuming manual monitoring and maintenance
  • Lack of data-driven insights for optimization
  • Complex and unintuitive existing solutions

Key Research Findings:

  • 92% of users wanted real-time monitoring capabilities
  • 87% expressed interest in automated watering systems
  • 76% needed better data visualization tools
  • 68% wanted mobile access to their systems

Wireframes

The wireframing process focused on creating an intuitive layout that would make complex data easily digestible. Key considerations included:

  • Clear hierarchy of information
  • Intuitive navigation between different plant zones
  • Easy access to crucial controls and data
  • Mobile-first responsive design

Multiple iterations were created and tested with users to ensure the final layout met both functional and usability requirements.

Final Design

The final design implements a clean, modern interface that emphasizes data visualization and quick access to essential controls. Key features include:

  • Real-time monitoring dashboard with customizable widgets
  • Automated watering and feeding schedules
  • Historical data analysis and trend visualization
  • Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go monitoring
  • Smart notifications and alerts system
  • Comprehensive plant health tracking
