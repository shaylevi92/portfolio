iPlant: Smart Plant Monitoring System

A revolutionary IoT solution for optimal plant growth and monitoring

Project Overview

iPlant is an innovative IoT solution that helps users monitor and maintain their plants' health through advanced sensors and machine learning algorithms. The system provides real-time data about soil moisture, light levels, and temperature, enabling optimal growing conditions.

Role

  • UX/UI Design
  • Frontend Development
  • IoT Integration

Technologies

  • React Native
  • Node.js
  • Arduino
  • Machine Learning

Process & Challenges

Research & Discovery

Extensive research was conducted to understand plant care needs and user pain points. This included interviews with plant enthusiasts and professional gardeners.

Design & Prototyping

The design process focused on creating an intuitive interface that would make plant care accessible to everyone, from beginners to experts.

Development & Testing

The development phase involved creating both the hardware and software components, ensuring seamless integration between sensors and the mobile application.
