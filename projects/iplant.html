My Agriculture Story

As a son of a farming family, the world of agriculture was always an inseparable part of my life. In my childhood, I worked in the family greenhouses together with my grandfather and father during summer vacations.

Farmers work tirelessly to maintain optimal growing conditions, leading to high operational costs and physical strain.

The Solution

AI-Powered Monitoring

Real-time monitoring and control using artificial intelligence that learns farmer preferences.

Smart Automation

Personalized action scenarios for ventilation, irrigation, and lighting systems.

Resource Optimization

Improved efficiency in water, electricity, and labor management.

Final Design

Interactive dashboard with real-time monitoring and control features.
