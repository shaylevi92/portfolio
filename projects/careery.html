Careery: AI-Powered Career Development Platform

Empowering career growth through personalized AI guidance and skill development

Project Overview

Careery is an innovative AI-powered platform that helps users navigate their career path through personalized guidance, skill assessments, and targeted learning recommendations.

Role

  • UX/UI Design
  • Frontend Development
  • AI Integration

Technologies

  • React
  • Node.js
  • OpenAI API
  • Machine Learning

Process & Challenges

Research & Discovery

Extensive market research and user interviews were conducted to understand the pain points in career development and how AI could address them effectively.

Design & Development

The platform was designed with a focus on user experience, making complex career guidance accessible and actionable through an intuitive interface.

AI Integration

Integration of advanced AI models to analyze career paths, skill gaps, and provide personalized recommendations based on individual goals and market demands.
